FUNNY FACTS ABOUT LEOPARDS





  • Leopards are excellent climbers and can carry their prey up into trees for safekeeping, like a takeaway meal!


  • They are known for their spots, but did you know that a group of spots on a leopard's fur is called a "rosette"?


  • Leopards are quite secretive and elusive, earning them the nickname "the phantom of the jungle."
    •

  • Despite their fearsome reputation, leopards can be quite playful, especially when they're young.


  • Leopards are solitary creatures, but they are quite vocal, and their distinctive raspy call is often described as a "sawing" sound.


  • When leopards are content and relaxed, they might purr just like domestic cats do.


  • Leopards are strong swimmers and have been spotted paddling in water bodies to cool off or catch fish.

  • In certain cultures, leopards have been believed to have mythical powers, adding to their mystique.


  • Leopards are incredibly agile and can jump distances of up to 20 feet in a single leap.


  • They have a unique way of storing their kills in trees, which helps them avoid scavengers on the ground.
