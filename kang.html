FUNNY FACTS ABOUT KANGAROOS





  • Kangaroos are marsupials, which means they carry their young in a pouch. It's like a built-in kangaroo daycare!


  • Kangaroos can hop at impressive speeds, reaching up to 40 miles per hour. They would definitely give Usain Bolt a run for his money!


  • Kangaroos have an interesting way of grooming themselves. They lick their forearms and then use them to clean their face, almost like a kangaroo spa treatment!


  • You might think they are big jumpers, but did you know that kangaroos can't jump backward? They are forward-hopping experts.
    •

  • A group of kangaroos is called a "mob." Can you imagine witnessing a mob of hopping kangaroos?


  • Kangaroos are herbivores and mainly eat grass, plants, and leaves. You won't find any kangaroos raiding a fridge for snacks!
    •

  • Kangaroos have a unique way of drinking water. They use their lips to create a seal around the water source and then suck it up like a straw.
    •


  • They have powerful tails that they use for balance and support while hopping. It's like a built-in pogo stick!


  • Kangaroos have a keen sense of smell, which helps them find food and detect predators from a distance.


  • Baby kangaroos, called joeys, are born the size of a jellybean and crawl into their mother's pouch to continue growing and developing. It's a tiny start to becoming a big and bouncy kangaroo!
