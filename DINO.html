  • The name "Brontosaurus" means "thunder lizard," but it turns out it never actually existed as a separate species. It was just a mix-up of fossils!


  • There was a dinosaur called Microraptor that had feathers and was the size of a crow. It was like a tiny dinosaur version of a ninja bird!


  • Dinosaurs may have been colorfully adorned, but we can only guess their colors based on fossil evidence.

  • The Stegosaurus had a brain the size of a walnut, making it one of the smallest brains relative to body size among dinosaurs.
    •

  • Some dinosaurs could lay up to hundreds of eggs at once, just like some modern-day reptiles.
    •

  • T-Rex's arms were so short that it couldn't scratch its own nose!
    •


  • The Triceratops had a massive frill on its head, but it probably didn't use it as a serving tray for snacks as some cartoons suggest.

  • The "velociraptors" in the Jurassic Park movies were much larger and not as smart as the real ones. They were about the size of a turkey in reality
