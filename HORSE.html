FUNNY FACTS ABOUT HORSES





  • Horses can sleep both lying down and standing up, which is pretty unusual for animals.


  • Did you know that a horse's teeth can take up more space in their head than their brain?


  • Horses have an impressive memory; they can remember a person's face for years.


  • A horse's heart is massive, weighing around 10 pounds, and it beats about 30 to 40 times per minute.
    •

  • Horses are social animals and can form strong bonds with other horses and even non-equine companions like goats or dogs.
    •

  • Have you ever heard a horse laugh? Well, they can! It's a unique and funny sound they make called "neighing."
    •

  • Horses are sensitive to human emotions; they can sense fear and respond accordingly.
    •

  • Horses can't vomit, so when they have an upset stomach, they have to deal with it.


  • If you see a horse yawning, don't worry; it doesn't mean they're bored. Horses yawn to help regulate their body temperature.


  • Horseback riding can be a great workout. You can burn up to 300 calories per hour just by riding a horse!
