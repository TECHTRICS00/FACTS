FUNNY FACTS ABOUT RAT





  • Rats laugh when they're tickled! It's an ultrasonic noise that humans can't hear without special equipment.


  • Rats have an amazing sense of smell, so they can easily detect new foods and even landmines.


  • They're excellent swimmers and can hold their breath for up to three minutes.


  • Rats are highly social creatures and love to play with each other, even wrestling for fun.
    •

  • Their front teeth never stop growing, which means they have to gnaw on things to keep them in check.
    •

  • Rats are incredible escape artists, and they can squeeze through holes the size of a quarter.
    •

  • They're known to be very clean animals and will spend hours grooming themselves and their fellow rats.



  • Rats are super smart and can learn tricks, solve puzzles, and navigate mazes with ease.
    •

  • In some cultures, rats are considered symbols of wealth and prosperity.
    •

  • They have an excellent memory and can remember their way around complicated paths even after weeks of not being there.
