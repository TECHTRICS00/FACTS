FUNNY FACTS ABOUT RABBIT





  • Rabbits have 18 toes - they have four toes on each of their front paws and two toes on each of their back paws.
    •

  • A group of rabbits is called a "fluffle" or a "colony."


  • Rabbits can jump up to three feet high and cover a distance of up to 10 feet in a single leap.


  • They have 360-degree panoramic vision, allowing them to detect predators from almost any direction.
    •

  • A rabbit's teeth never stop growing, so they need to chew on things constantly to wear them down


  • They are crepuscular animals, meaning they are most active during dawn and dusk.
    •

  • Rabbits can purr, just like cats, when they are content or relaxed.
    •


  • A rabbit's sense of smell is incredibly keen, and they use it to identify food and detect danger.


  • Rabbits are notorious for their love of carrots, thanks to Bugs Bunny, but in reality, carrots should be given to them in moderation due to their high sugar content.
    •

  • Some rabbits exhibit a behavior called "binky," which is a joyful jump and twist in the air, often seen when they are excited or happy.
